THE CRAFT

Every piece begins with something you never want to forget — a wedding varmala, a handwritten letter, a first bloom. It’s cleaned, arranged by hand, and set in layers of crystal-clear epoxy resin that cure over days, not minutes.

Nothing here is mass-produced. Colours shift, petals settle, and each cast carries the small imperfections that prove a human made it — for you.
